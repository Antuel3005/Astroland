Rides & Attractions

Power Tower

Feel the adrenaline as you plunge 240 feet, experiencing extreme freefall and breathtaking views of the park below.

WindSeeker

Soar 301 feet in the air, gently spinning with panoramic views, perfect for a thrilling yet serene flying experience.

Scrambler

A classic family favorite, twist and turn at high speeds as the Scrambler delivers an exhilarating, dizzying ride experience.

SlingShot

Launch 360 feet into the air at 62 mph, defying gravity with intense speed and thrilling weightlessness sensations.
