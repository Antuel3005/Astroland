FAQs

Is there a dress code?

No, there is no dress code but it is recommended as it is a lot more fun to be an AstroKid!

Can I leave the park and re-enter?

Yes. As you exit, you will need to receive a re-entry ticket from the exit associate.

If I’ve lost something at the park, what do I do?

If you’ve lost something, you may report it missing online or on one of the computers at Guest Services. You must fill out a missing item report in order for your item to be returned if found. Once the report is submitted, you will be sent updates on your item. Please note that if you’ve lost an item on one of our rides, our ride hosts may not be able to retrieve it immediately.
